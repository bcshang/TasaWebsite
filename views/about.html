Welcome!

Whatever it is at TASA that brought you to join or even look at our website, whether it's our famous annual night market, our events such as dumpling night, our general meetings, or even a friend who is actively part of TASA, we encourage you to join us to continue making TASA what it is. We welcome everyone and will always strive to promote culture and diversity, as well as be a friendly group of officers and interns.

Our Mission

The Taiwanese American Students Association at the University of Texas - Austin is a social/cultural/philanthropic organization dedicated to providing a sense of community for ethnic Taiwanese, as well as those interested in Taiwanese culture. We strive to create a sense of unity not only within the Taiwanese community here in Austin but also with students at other universities through the nationwide Intercollegiate Taiwanese American Students Association. Our organization nurtures cultural awareness by building a community of motivated and dedicated students through our social, cultural, and volunteer activities that take place throughout the year.
